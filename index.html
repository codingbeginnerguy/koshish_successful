about mussut

We bring your needs closer to you!

During pandemic, we saw struggles local businesses were going through while online systems were thriving. While our local businesses had all consumer needs, there was a lack of standard point. And this is how the idea of Mussut was born.


Our Goal Is To Re-Imagine The Way That The Common Man Shops And Act As A Bridge Between The Customer And The Market, Making The Process Smooth For Both Parties. We aim to make Indian Local markets empowered and give a seamless transition in the era of digitalization.
