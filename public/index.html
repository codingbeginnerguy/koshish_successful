about mussut

Easy And Perfect Solution For All Your Needs.

We are a hyper-local platform developed in India to empower small businesses and create an even playing field for all shop owners, small or big. Our goal is to re-imagine the way that the common man shops and act as a bridge between the customer and the market, making the process smooth for both parties. We identified the problems that local shopkeepers faced in the incredibly fast-moving and evolving market and decided to support them at a grass root level to save the "Gali ki Dukans" from going the way of the Dodos.

Why Mussut

Fast Delivery
Fast Delivery

Everything delivered with lightening fast speed is our motto and this is easily made possible by collaborating with the local shops you know and love.

Equal Opportunities
Equal Opportunities

The famous online retailers are dominated by a handful of big suppliers who have huge leverage over the prices due to the large volume of products they move every month. This has pushed the smaller sellers to the brink of extinction, we aim to bring them back.

Product Comparison
Product Comparison

You can easily compare products based on quality and price from all the shops near you as they are all available on Mussut and this prevents them from trying to fool anyone into higher prices and keeps the market fair for the customer as well as the shopkeepers.

Vast Range
Vast Range

We do not realise the choices that are available in a local market unless we spend a whole day hunting for a certain product. Now the hunting is done by us for you and the result is a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience.

Local Economy
Local Economy

If everything is bought from the local markets and delivered to the customers instead of buying it from across the country and having it delivered, the Local economy definitely gets a boost that is needed at this time.
